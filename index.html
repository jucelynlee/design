I'm a Commercial Designer who solves business challenges through compelling visual storytelling.

[ Profile ]

With over two years of commercial experience, I specialize in crafting high-conversion digital assets for global tech and consumer brands, including JLab, EarFun, myFirst, and Cricut. By embracing modern AI workflows—seamlessly integrating tools like GPT and Gemini—I translate complex requirements into scalable, data-driven design systems. I don't just make things look good; I build memorable brand identities that drive tangible commercial growth.

在思博特任職期間，我負責為 JLab、EarFun、myFirst 與 Cricut 等國際知名 3C 品牌，打造具備高轉換率的產品落地頁與社群素材。面對不斷演進的 AI 趨勢，我已將 GPT 與 Gemini 深度整合至設計工作流中，將繁雜的設計需求，系統化地轉變為高擴展性的視覺資產。我不僅僅是做設計，更致力於塑造令人難忘的品牌識別，進而帶動實質的商業成長。
