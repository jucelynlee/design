01
Brand Identity & Visual System
I define bespoke color palettes and visual identities rooted in product cores and brand storytelling. Whether building from scratch or extending established brand guidelines, I create cohesive online and offline key visuals that accurately engage the target market.
從產品核心與品牌故事出發，為品牌定調專屬色系與視覺形象。無論是從零建構，或是基於原廠規範延伸品牌設計系統，皆能打造出具高度一致性且精準對接市場的線上與線下活動主視覺。
02
E-Commerce & Digital Marketing
Focused on creating digital assets that drive clicks and conversions. With extensive experience handling distributor and agency brands, I design eye-catching promotional banners and landing pages, flexibly adapting and resizing assets across various e-commerce platforms and channel specifications.
專注於創造點擊與轉換的數位視覺。具備經手多項代理與經銷品牌的實戰經驗，能針對不同電商平台與檔期需求，設計高吸睛度的各式廣告圖與新品介紹頁，並靈活應對跨通路規範進行版位尺寸延伸與調整。
03
Social Media & Motion Graphics
Crafting scroll-stopping visual content for social feeds. I develop visual strategies and daily posts for various social platforms, and handle end-to-end short-form video production—from shooting to editing—amplifying brand presence through a dynamic mix of media.
打造在社群動態上有效留客的視覺內容。熟稔各類通訊與社群平台的視覺策略與日常貼文設計，並能一手包辦短影音的拍攝與剪輯，以動靜態素材的靈活交錯，全面提升品牌的線上聲量。

Translating visual concepts into tangible physical experiences. Drawing on international market experience, I design retail packaging tailored for US audiences. My scope covers shipping boxes, promotional flyers, exhibition booths, and strict print quality control.
將視覺概念完美落地為實體體驗。具備跨國市場與展會實務經驗，專責設計針對美國受眾的零售包裝。此外，設計範疇亦涵蓋產品物流紙箱、宣傳單、大型展座及各式印刷物料，並嚴密把關最終輸出品質。
